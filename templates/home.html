Project Summary

We used a classification model to try to accurately predict the ESRB ratings for games that have recently come out and are not listed in our data set. We used Python/Pandas to clean the data we got from Kaggle, and then trained our model based on the dataset. The dataset only contained ESRB ratings for PS4 and Xbox One Games so we also tested the model to see how accurately it could predict game ratings for games based on other platforms.


Research Questions to Answer:
1. How accurate is our model at predicting the ESRB for newer video games that aren’t in our original dataset?
2. What is the reason for any inaccuracy in the predictions?
3. Can this model be applied to games for other platforms that aren’t present in our original dataset?
